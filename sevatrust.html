A New Chapter in Public Service

In 2021, Atul Rai embarked on a new chapter of his life by establishing the Rameshwaram Seva Trust in Handia, Harda District, Madhya Pradesh. This initiative represents his unwavering commitment to public welfare and social service.

The trust focuses on three core areas: environmental protection, strengthening the Indian family system, and providing compassionate service to patients. Every initiative undertaken by the trust reflects the belief that true service lies in alleviating the suffering of others and building a more harmonious society.
