Political Journey

BJP Madhya Pradesh

Leveraging his extensive organizational experience, Atul Rai took on a key responsibility in the political sphere. From 2016 to 2019, he served as the Co-Organization General Secretary for the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) in Madhya Pradesh.

During this period, he was noted for successfully organizing large-scale events in smaller towns and overseeing the construction of well-regarded party offices. In politics, where making decisions while staying firm on one's ideology can be difficult, he continued to apply the principles and methods learned from his time in the Sangh to guide his work and actions.
