About Atul Rai

Atul Rai was born on April 24, 1979, in Maunath Bhanjan, Uttar Pradesh. He spent his childhood and received his education in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, completing his early schooling at Ramkrishna Vidya Mandir and graduating with a B.Com from MLB College. He grew up with his parents and a younger sister.

His interests include music, acting, public speaking, and the culinary arts. Those who know him describe him as a strong administrator and a natural leader who is also forgiving, sociable, and punctual. His personal ideology is rooted in friendship, humor, truthfulness, and the concept of "वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम" – the world is one family.
