Journey with

Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS)

Atul Rai's association with the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) began during his school days. This early start led to a lifetime of dedication, and in July 2000, he became a full-time Sangh Pracharak, a role he served in until December 2021. Over these two decades, he took on various responsibilities for the Sangh in diverse locations, including Gwalior, Bhopal, Sehore, Rajgarh, Vidisha, and Jammu & Kashmir.

He notes that the work in Jammu & Kashmir was comparatively more difficult than in other parts of India. He approached this challenge by applying the Sangh's core ideology and unique working style to make the task easier. A formative experience from his youth was doing service work in a Naxal-affected tribal area in the 9th grade through the NCC, which solidified his commitment to service.
